How much does a custom website cost for a startup?
Unlike traditional web agencies that quote $8,000–$20,000 with bloated overhead, our custom website development for startups starts at a transparent flat fee starting at $1,500 (or localized ₹14,999 INR rates). You get a 100% custom-built design, mobile-first engineering, enterprise-grade security hardening, and 3 months of free post-launch support included.
Should I choose a website builder vs custom web development?
Website builders (like generic templates or drag-and-drop tools) leave your startup looking like thousands of competitors, load slowly with code bloat, and break down as you scale. Custom website design for non-technical founders gives your brand distinct authority, lightning-fast conversion speeds, and complete technical flexibility without requiring you to manage code.
Should I hire a freelance web developer for my startup or work with WebArch?
When you hire a lone web developer, you often risk missed deadlines, subpar UI/UX design, or abandoned code post-launch. WebArch operates as a dedicated website design agency for small business and startup founders—giving you senior designers and engineers working in parallel, guaranteed 14-day delivery, and our signature 3 months of free post-launch maintenance.
How do you deliver custom website design with a fast turnaround?
We are a web agency that builds websites fast without cutting corners. By eliminating account managers, unnecessary revision loops, and recycled layouts, our team sprints from discovery to live deployment in just 14 days (or up to a custom website design 3 week turnaround for complex web application features).
Do you offer website design with ongoing support included?
Absolutely. Every project includes 3 months of free post-launch support with zero asterisks. We handle bug fixes, performance tuning, content updates, and security patches so first-time founders and small business owners can focus 100% on scaling their venture.
Which regions and types of founders do you work with?
We partner with all types of founders worldwide—including technical solo creators, first-time non-technical entrepreneurs, DTC e-commerce brands, SaaS scale-ups, and agency owners. We serve clients across all regions globally, including the US, UK, Canada, Australia, Europe, India, Singapore, and Latin America with region-tailored pricing.
